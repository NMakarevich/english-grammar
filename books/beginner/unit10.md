## 1. Grammar Spot

1. Irregular verbs: <br>
got up (get up) <br>
had (has/have) <br>
saw (see) <br>
went (go) <br>
met (meet) <br>
bought (buy) <br>
did (do) 
2. Regular verbs: <br>
cook<b>ed</b> <br>
play<b>ed</b> <br>
start<b>ed</b> <br>
watch<br>ed</b> <br>
listen<b>ed</b> <br>
invit<b>ed</b> 
3. The Past Simple is the same for _I/you/he/she/it/we/they_ <br>
I/You/He/She/It/We/They **played/went**

## 2. Grammar Spot

**Present Simple questions and negatives**
1. Questions and negatives use **do** and **does** <br>
**Do** you **like** golf? No, I **don't**. <br>
**Does** she **like** golf? No, she **doesn't**.

**Past Simple questions and negatives**
2. All questions use **did** <br>
**Did** she **have** a good weekend? Where **did** the play? <br>
What **did** you **do** yesterday? When **did** you go?
3. All negatives use **didn't**.
He **didn't want** to stay in bed. <br>
They **didn't go out**. <br>
I **didn't cook** a meal.

## 3. Grammar Spot

**_ago_** <br>
**Two years ago** means **two years before now** <br>
I met James **ten years ago**. <br>
I went to New York **six weeks ago**. <br>
Sally phoned **five minutes ago**.

### 4.2 Past Simple questions and negatives
> [!NOTE]
> Present -> Past <br>
> **do/does** -> **did** <br>
> What time **does** he usually **get up**? <br>
> What time **did** he **get up** yesterday?

**Question with question words**

|       |         |                                                |         |
|-------|---------|------------------------------------------------|---------|
| Where | **did** | I<br/> you <br/> he/she/it <br/> we <br/> they | **go**? |

**Negative**

|                                                 |            |                                          |
|-------------------------------------------------|------------|------------------------------------------|
| I <br/> You <br/> He/She/It <br/> We <br/> They | **didn't** | **go** shopping. <br/> **see** the film. |

**Yes/No questions and short answers**

|                                   |                    |
|-----------------------------------|--------------------|
| **Did** they **play** football?   | Yes, they **did**. |
| **Did** you **have** a good time? | No, I **didn't**.  |
