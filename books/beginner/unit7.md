## 1. Grammar reference

### 1.1 Question words

What? - A hamburger. <br>
What time? - At 8.00. <br>
Where? - In New York. <br>
When? - On Sunday. <br>
Who? - Peter. <br>
Why? - Because... <br>
How? - By taxi. <br>
How old? - Nearly seven. <br>
How many? - Ten students. <br>
How much? - $2.

### 1.2 Pronouns

| Subject        | I  | you  | he  | she | it  | we | they  |
|----------------|----|------|-----|-----|-----|----|-------|
| **Object**     | me | you  | him | her | it  | us | them  |
| **Possessive** | my | your | his | her | its | us | their |

**She** loves **him**. <br>
**I** like **your** car.

### 1.3 this/that

We use _this_ to refer to things near to us.

**This** is my son. <br>
I like **this** sandwich. <br>

We use _that_ to refer to things that are not near to us.

**That**'s my house. <br>
I don't like **that** car.
