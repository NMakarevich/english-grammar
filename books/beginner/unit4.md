## 1. Grammar Spot

1. He<b>'s</b> married.  He<b>'s</b> a teacher. **'s** = **is**
2. This is Ian<b>'s</b> family. **'s** = the family of Ian.
3. Ian's/his  school
4. her/Jenny's hospital

## 4. Grammar reference

### 4.1 Possessive adjectives

### 4.2 Possessive 's

_'s_ shows possession.

This is John. This is his bike. -> This is John<b>'s</b> bike. <br>
This is Marie. This is her саг. -> This is Marie<b>'s</b> car. <br>
Кеllу and Tim are married. This is their son. -> This is Kelly and Tim<b>'s</b> son.

his house -> Noel<b>'s</b> house <br>
her name -> your wife<b>'s</b> name <br>
their daughter -> Pam and Bob<b>'s</b> daughter

> [!NOTE]
> _'s_ is also the short form of _is_

he<b>'s</b> = he **is** <br>
she<b>'s</b> = she **is** <br>
it<b>'s</b> = it **is** <br>
Who<b>'s</b> = Who is
