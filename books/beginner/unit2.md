1. Grammar Spot

he<b>'s</b> = he is

she<b>'s</b> = she is

Male pronouns: he, his

Female pronouns: she, her

2. Grammar Spot

Where<b>'s</b> = Where is

Where **is** she from?

Where **is** he from?

Where **are** you from?

5. Grammar reference

5.1 Possessive adjectives

**My** name's Serena.

What's **your** name?

**His** name's Antonio.

What's **her** name?

his = possessive adjective

**his** name, **his** bike, **his** watch

he's = he is

**He's** Bruno. **He's** from Brazil. **He's** fine.
