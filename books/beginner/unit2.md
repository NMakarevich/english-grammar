## 1. Grammar Spot

he<b>'s</b> = he is <br>
she<b>'s</b> = she is <br>
Male pronouns: he, his <br>
Female pronouns: she, her

## 2. Grammar Spot

Where<b>'s</b> = Where is

Where **is** she from? <br>
Where **is** he from? <br>
Where **are** you from?

## 3. Grammar Spot

## 5. Grammar reference

### 5.1 Possessive adjectives

**My** name's Serena. <br>
What's **your** name? <br>
**His** name's Antonio. <br>
What's **her** name? <br>

his = possessive adjective

**his** name, **his** bike, **his** watch

he's = he is

**He's** Bruno. **He's** from Brazil. **He's** fine.

5.2 Questions with question words

|         |                                  |                                      |
|---------|----------------------------------|--------------------------------------|
