## 1. Grammar Spot

He<b>'s</b> a student. **'s** = is <br>
She **isn't** a nurse. **isn't** = is not

## 2. Grammar Spot

1. Negative

**I'm not** a nurse.            **I'm not** = I am not <br>
**They aren't** from England.   **They aren't** = They are not <br>
**We aren't** teachers.         **We aren't** = We are not

2. Short answers

Are you from Glasgow? <br>
**Yes, I am.**/**No, I'm not.** <br>
**Yes, we are.**/**No, we aren't.**

Is this your football team? <br>
**Yes, it is.**/**No, it isn't.**

Are they from England? <br>

**Yes, they are.**/**No, they aren't.**

## 3. Grammar Spot

**We're** in Paris. **we're** = we are
